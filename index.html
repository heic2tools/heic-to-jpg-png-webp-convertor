    How it works

    1. 01

      Select your HEIC files

      Drag and drop, or click to browse. Batch conversion is supported.

    2. 02

      Pick an output format

      JPG for compatibility, PNG for transparency, WebP for smaller size.

    3. 03

      Download instantly

      Everything runs locally in your browser using JavaScript — nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

    Privacy by design

    Most online converters upload your photos to a server to process them. heic2.tools doesn't. Conversion happens entirely in your browser's memory using the heic2any library — your files are never transmitted anywhere.
